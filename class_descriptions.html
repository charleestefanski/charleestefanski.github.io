Class Descriptions

Hot Vinyasa

This is a high-energy, high-intensity class that is paired with fist-pumping, booty shaking music. The room is heated, and sweat will start to pour! Get your heart racing with a mixture of dynamic and static movements.

Slow Burn

This class is all about feelz. We will take you through some slower, more grounded movements. The room will still be heated, but the intensity of the class comes from you, not the heater. We challenge all students in this class to close their eyes or even blindfold themselves in order to truly experience every sensation.

Yin

This class takes it all the way down. It is filled with long-holding poses that are meant to unravel you. You will learn how to slow your breath down, and how to breath into every sensation that rises to the surface. Close your eyes, take a breath, and settle in.

Follow the Yogi (Ashtanga)

Move with one of our baller teachers as they take you through their daily Ashtanga practice! The movements will be more regimented and guided, but there will still be some personal twists thrown in there to keep you on your toes! This class is perfect for beginners who have never practiced before, for pros who want to strengthen their yoga roots, and for anyone in between!

Yinyasa

A perfect blend of vinyasa and yin, this class takes you through dynamic flows and long-holding postures. The fast-paced movements combined with the long-holding postures will leave you feeling like jelly in the best way possible.
